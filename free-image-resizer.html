Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our image resizer is completely free to use. You can resize as many images as you want without any cost or limitations.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Currently, our tool supports resizing one image at a time. For batch processing, you would need to resize each image individually.

Does resizing affect image quality?

Resizing can affect quality, especially when making images larger. Our tool uses advanced algorithms to maintain the best possible quality during resizing.

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats. The resized image can be downloaded in the same format as the original.
