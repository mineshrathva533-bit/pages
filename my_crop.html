Frequently Asked Questions

Is the cropper free to use?

Yes, our image cropper is completely free to use. You can crop as many images as you want without any cost or limitations.

Can I crop images to a fixed aspect ratio?

Yes, our tool provides pre-defined aspect ratios like 1:1, 4:3, and 16:9, as well as a 'Free' option for custom sizes.

Does cropping reduce image quality?

Cropping itself does not reduce the quality of the selected area. You are simply removing pixels from the edges of the image. The original quality of the cropped section is preserved.

Do you store my images?

Absolutely not. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

Can I use the tool on mobile devices?

Yes, our online image cropper is designed to be fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
